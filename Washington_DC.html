Washington, DC

To most of the world, Washington, DC is the capitol of the United States of America. It is where federal congressmen gather to deliberate, making decisions, and represented the country. However, there is more to life than just politics.

Although there is much history in the area, it is also a location that representes the future. Washington, DC is full of youth, partially due to the presitgious colleges and job opportunities in the region. The DC area is also home to various cultural attractions such as museums, theater, and nighttime life.
